How It Works

Clarity in 3 steps

1

Dump everything on your mind

Tasks, goals, ideas, obligations — don't organize, just talk. Text or voice. The AI understands context and sorts it for you.

2

Set what matters this week or month

Tell the app what success looks like. "File my taxes and get back into running." That becomes the lens for everything.

3

Get told what to do — not a list, a decision

Every day, the app picks the 1-2 things that matter most. If something starts slipping, it nudges you. If you've been ignoring something, it asks: still want this?

Quick Setup

Bring your own AI (it's free)

Drift uses Google's Gemini AI. You'll need a free API key — takes about 60 seconds.

1

Go to Google AI Studio

Visit aistudio.google.com/apikey and sign in with your Google account.

2

Create an API key

Click "Create API Key", pick any project (or create one), and copy the key.

3

Paste it in Drift

Open the app → tap the ⚙️ icon on the Today screen → paste your key → Save. That's it.

The free tier is generous — more than enough for personal use. Your key stays on your device and is only sent to Google's API.
